Definition?

IMPROVE, BETTER, HELP, AMELIORATE mean to make more acceptable or to bring nearer a standard. IMPROVE and BETTER are general and interchangeable and apply to what can be made better whether it is good or bad. Asking or seeking for help is not a weakness or failure it's a zeal and "willingness" to get better and improve!
